About Cash flow Tracker

Who We Are

Cash flow Tracker is a web-based solution designed to empower small business owners in Tanzania. Our platform helps users effectively manage their finances by tracking income, expenses, and savings. With simple tools and clear visualizations, we aim to make financial tracking accessible and efficient for businesses earning between 100,000 TZS and 500,000 TZS.

Our Mission

To provide a user-friendly, reliable, and secure platform that enables small businesses to achieve better financial control and sustainable growth.

What We Offer

  • Real-time income and expense tracking
  • Confirmation of transactions from mobile vendors
  • Detailed financial reports
  • Visual summaries with charts and graphs
